Light commercial & small fleets

Your vehicles are how you earn a living. KARS keeps small fleets, work trucks, and commercial vehicles moving with practical, reliable repair and maintenance.

  • Preventative maintenance plans that match your workload, not just the calendar.
  • Priority on uptime, safety and real-world reliability.
  • Honest communication—repairs you need, not “extras” you don’t.
  • Welding and custom solutions for racks, hitches, suspension and more.

Upgrades with brands you recognize

KARS is a local source for performance, utility and protection upgrades using brands their customers already know and ask for:

  • Air Lift – dealer & installer for load support and leveling.
  • Rev-X – treatment and additives trusted for engines and drivetrains.
  • KC HiLites – lighting upgrades, installed correctly.
  • WeatherTech & Thule – protection and cargo systems for real use.
  • Bilstein – shock and suspension upgrades.

Tell them how you use your vehicle—daily driver, tow rig, work truck or trail rig—and they’ll help pick the right upgrades instead of just the flashiest catalog options.
